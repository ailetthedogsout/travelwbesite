Our Mission

At VisitNorway, our mission is to inspire and guide travelers to experience the breathtaking beauty and rich culture of Norway. We strive to provide comprehensive information and personalized travel recommendations to ensure every visitor has an unforgettable adventure.

Our History

Founded in 2005, VisitNorway has grown from a small startup into one of the leading travel information websites dedicated to Norway. Our team of passionate travel experts works tirelessly to share the wonders of our country with the world.

Core Values

We believe in sustainable tourism, respecting local cultures, and promoting environmental conservation. Our values guide every aspect of our work as we aim to create positive impacts on the communities and environments we highlight.
